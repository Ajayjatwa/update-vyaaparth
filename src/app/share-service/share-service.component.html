Shared or Integrated services

To scale their business, manage costs and improve customer satisfaction, organizations need to optimize their internal resources under pressure from boards and investors.


a)) Establish a vision for service delivery in the future.

Decentralized, heritage operating models can perpetuate unproductive and unnecessary spending. With Vyaaparth, we work with you to assess your current service delivery model and develop a plan that aligns with the company's goals and objectives. We look at how processes, organizational structures, and technology must evolve to support your vision in the long run.


b)) Automate where possible

Automation is not just a way of doing the same thing faster or cheaper; it enables you to do it differently and achieve better results. We help you assess your current operations to identify process improvements that can be automated and standardized so that you can reduce manual effort and promote consistency throughout your business.


c)) Update your core technology applications

With access to technology, you can speed up the transformation of your service delivery practices.

We combine technology and strategy to give you a competitive edge. Our experts work with you to help you implement analytics, customer-facing systems, and administrative systems that form the infrastructure that supports innovation.


d)) Encourage career advancement to retain talent

Employee engagement is essential to delivering consistently high-quality services. Leadership teams can rethink staffing models and organizational structures to serve their business goals better. Working together with you, we'll align employees around common goals and duties to provide standardized service across the organization and receive new growth opportunities.


e)) Make accountability a priority

Administrators are often asked to demonstrate their value to the business units they support. Our company works with leadership teams to outline a standard service-level agreement that sets out clear expectations for the back-office staff and customers they serve. Additionally, we assist you in defining the performance metrics you will need to quantify progress towards those goals and ensure accountability across your organization.
