Operation of Sales- Transformation

The role of Sales Operations is to serve as a lynchpin between Marketing, Sales, Service, and Finance and to drive predictable and profitable growth in today's digital economy. Over 600 service management leaders were surveyed globally about digital transformation across 20 industries: "What does digital transformation mean to your organization?"? Digital transformations, according to 52% of respondents, are about sharing more information about their products and services through better websites. The majority group members did not discuss integrating the entire sales process to allow commerce transactions to run smoothly or entirely reimagine the whole procedure.


One way to gain a deeper understanding of the digitalization of sales operations is to look at the 8 percent of sales organizations that report expected or better results from digital transformation.


The sales process starts with positioning, value articulation, and integration with Marketing, followed by smooth, easy, and minimalistic digital sales processes. An effective sales operations team supports this integrated transactional capability, planning, organizing budgets, enabling and monitoring the performance of sales channels across the organization.


By leveraging our digital sales expertise, you will be able to transform your company into a predictive, integrated, and KPI-driven sales operation.


It isn't enough for organizations to build great products and services- they must make it easy for their customers to purchase them repeatedly. Additionally, great sales organizations operate like well-oiled machines with process digitalization and integration from back-end to front-end. Furthermore, successful organizations know that they are part of a chain, beginning with Marketing, extending into service, and providing feedback to R&D/Product Innovation.


As a sales operations partner, Vyaaparth works on these themes with clients.

Process integration for Marketing, Sales, and Service

Creating a fast, easy, and one-stop shopping experience for customers is the main focus. Customers and the sales organization must be seamlessly integrated into tools and processes such as customer lead generation, quote-to-order conversion, product-to-service conversion, etc.

Deliver value in a fully integrated manner

Customers in the digital economy need to know when a product or service is delivered during the purchase process. Consequently, integrating last-mile fulfillment providers into the sales process is critical to driving customer purchases.

A sales culture that is data-driven and predictive

Digital transformations cannot be successful without analytics. To predict where sales are heading and what influences them, you should invest in Revenue, Customer, Channel, Product, Service, and Performance analytics.

Establish an adaptable sales organization

Creating flexible digital-physical channels, organizations, and territories is essential in the early stages of digital transformation until the data tells you what is effective and what is not.

Connect R&D, Marketing, and Innovation

Successful businesses are constantly improving. These two functions must share customer, product, service, and competitor information to differentiate their offerings. To drive successful transformations, our Sales operations digitalization practice focuses on the following areas.
